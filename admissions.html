Admissions

Admissions

Join the Faculty of Computing at Madonna University and begin your journey towards a successful career in technology.

Entry Requirements

Choose your entry mode based on your qualifications.

UTME Entry

4 Years Duration

  • Five O'Level credits including Mathematics and English Language
  • Credits must include Mathematics and English Language
  • Credit in Physics or any Science subject
  • Acceptable JAMB UTME score
  • JAMB subject combinations: Mathematics, Physics, and any two from Chemistry, Biology, Economics, Geography

Direct Entry

Varies

  • Two A-Level passes in relevant subjects
  • Minimum of 5 O'Level credits including Mathematics and English Language
  • ND/HND in Computer Science or related field with minimum Upper Credit
  • B.Sc in related discipline from accredited institution
  • Pass in JAMB UTME with relevant subject combinations

Transfer Entry

Varies

  • Transfer from other recognized universities
  • Transcript showing courses taken and grades
  • Minimum CGPA of 2.5 on a 5-point scale
  • Letter of attestation from former institution
  • Must meet UTME entry requirements

Application Process

Follow these simple steps to apply for admission.

1

Create Account

Visit the university portal and create an applicant account.

2

Complete Application

Fill in your personal, educational, and JAMB details.

3

Upload Documents

Upload scanned copies of required documents.

4

Pay Application Fee

Make payment using available payment options.

5

Submit Application

Review and submit your completed application.

6

Track Status

Monitor your application status on the portal.
