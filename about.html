About the Faculty of Computing

Discover our history, mission, and commitment to producing world-class technology professionals.

Our History & Vision

The Faculty of Computing at Madonna University was established with the vision of creating a center of excellence for technology education in Nigeria. Since our inception, the faculty has been at the forefront of producing graduates with relevant IT professional skills that are making significant contributions to the global tech industry.

With a team of dedicated and experienced lecturers, relevant equipment to prepare students for successful careers in computing and related fields. We are committed to research, innovation, and the development of solutions that address local and global challenges.

With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty members, and a curriculum that evolves with technological advancements, we continue to shape the future of computing education in Nigeria and beyond.

Our Mission

To provide quality education in computing sciences that produces graduates who are technically competent, ethically grounded, and equipped with the skills necessary for the advancement of society. We are committed to fostering innovation, research, and the development of solutions that address real-world challenges.

Our Vision

To be a leading faculty of computing in Africa and globally, recognized for excellence in teaching, research, and innovation. We aspire to produce world-class computing professionals who drive the global tech community, but also leaders and innovators who will shape the future of technology and contribute to sustainable development.
