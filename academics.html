Course Outline Structure

General Studies (GS)

Foundational courses covering communication skills, Nigerian history, and ethics. All students must complete these courses.

Core Courses

Department-specific courses that form the foundation of your degree program. These are mandatory courses.

Elective Courses

Optional courses allowing you to specialize in areas of interest and customize your learning path.

SIWES

Students Industrial Work Experience providing real-world experience in your field.

Final Year Project

Capstone project demonstrating practical application of your learning.

Examination Guidelines

All examinations are conducted at the end of each semester. Students must maintain a minimum of 75% class attendance to be eligible for examinations. Continuous assessment (CA) constitutes 30% of the total score, while final examinations constitute 70%.
